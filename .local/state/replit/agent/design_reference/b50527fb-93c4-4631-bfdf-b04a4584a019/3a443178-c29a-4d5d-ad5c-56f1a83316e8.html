Frequently Asked Questions

Is delivery discreet?

Yes, all deliveries are made in plain packaging with no identifying marks. We prioritize customer privacy and discretion.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, mobile money transfers, and bank transfers. Payment details will be discussed during order confirmation.

How do you ensure quality?

All products undergo strict quality control, proper storage conditions, and regular testing. We work only with trusted suppliers.

What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver throughout Myanmar including Yangon, Mandalay, Naypyidaw, and other major cities. Delivery times may vary by location.
